iRead — Support

iRead is a reading companion for learning languages: read foreign-language texts, tap any word to translate it, save it to your vocabulary, and review it later. Everything runs on your device, and your content syncs only through your own private iCloud.

Need help?

If you have a question, found a problem, or have a suggestion, email mishkatix88@gmail.com and I'll get back to you.

Common questions

Translation isn't working. Translation uses Apple's on-device system. The first time you use a language pair, your device may need to download it — make sure you're online for that initial download, then it works offline.

My words and texts aren't syncing between devices. Sign in to the same iCloud account on each device and make sure iCloud Drive is enabled. Syncing happens through your private iCloud and can take a little time.
